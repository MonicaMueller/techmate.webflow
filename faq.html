What is techmate?

Techmate is your own personal Tech Concierge. We provide technology support that comes to you, wherever you prefer. We are happy to help you set up new devices, troubleshoot issues, or teach you to use your new software or devices.

IS TECHMATE LICENSED AND INSURED?

Yes, Techmate is a California Corporation and is fully licensed and insured.

IS TECHMATE AVAILABLE IN MY AREA?

We currently serve The Bay Area (San Francisco, Oakland, and San Jose).

WHAT ARE YOUR HOURS?

Our Techmates are available between 9am and 9pm.

WHAT IS YOUR PROCESS FOR WORKING WITH A NEW CUSTOMER?

New customers can either book through the website or give us a call at (844) 266-8694.  We just need your location, the type of tech issue you need help with and when you’d like to set up an appointment. Then we’ll connect you with one of our expert Techmates, who will come to your home or office, or another location like a coffee shop, to perform the requested service.

WHY WAS TECHMATE STARTED?

One of our Founders, Colin Barceloux, came up with the idea for Techmate after an experience helping his father with a computer problem showed him how hard it can be to find safe, reliable in-home tech support. He realized there are many people like his father who need help with their computers or devices, but they don’t have time to stand in line at repair shops, wait a week for someone to come to their house, or even know who they can trust.

WHAT IF I’M NOT SURE WHAT THE ISSUE IS?

When booking your appointment through the website, simply select “something else” when you get to the device type and give us details in the “describe the issue” box.  If you have questions feel free to call or chat us for help.

HOW SOON CAN I SCHEDULE AN APPOINTMENT?

We can have a Techmate to your home in as little as 6 hours, assuming we have a Techmate available in your area.

WHY DO I NEED A CREDIT CARD WHILE BOOKING?

Techmate will only pre-authorize your card for the amount of the appointment, but you will not be fully charged until the the service is complete. A credit card secures your appointment and simplifies our Techmates duties. It also allows us to confirm your appointment at a specific time and date. They’ll handle the service and we’ll handle your payment so that your information stays safe and secure.

WHEN WILL MY TECHMATE ARRIVE?

Your techmate will be there at the time you book your appointment for. We know you don’t like arrival windows, and neither do we.

HOW WILL I IDENTIFY MY TECHMATE?

You will receive an email confirming the Techmate who has been assigned to you. The email will contain a clear photo of their face, and a little more information about them.

DO I NEED TO BE HOME?

Yes, we do require that you're home during the appointment.

HOW ARE YOUR TECHMATES SCREENED?

Our Techmates must pass a thorough background check, personality assessment, and knowledge test before joining the Techmate community of tech specialists.

HOW DO I REACH YOU?

You can either chat us through the website, email us at support@Techmate.com or give us a call at (844) 266-8694.

WHAT EDUCATION AND/OR TRAINING DO YOU HAVE THAT RELATES TO YOUR WORK?

All of our Techmates are tech support experts, with relevant training and experience for the types of devices they service. All of our Techmates must go through a background check, a personality test, and an in-person interview to qualify. Whether they specialize in troubleshooting home networks or repairing damaged smartphones and tablets, we only work with the experienced professionals.

HOW DO YOU PRICE YOUR SERVICES?

Most services are a flat fee of $139 per appointment. If your needs are more complex, we will reach out to you for more information and our best estimate of the service. If your needs turn out to be more complex than initially described, additional fees may apply. Your Techmates will discuss options with you.

HOW SHOULD I PREPARE FOR MY APPOINTMENT?

Give us as much information as you can about your needs so that we’re properly equipped to help when we arrive (details about what’s happening, as well as device types and model numbers). Other than that, make sure you have the power supplies for your devices, any relevant software, as well as account info and passwords if relevant for your appointment.

WHAT IF I NEED MORE HELP AFTER MY APPOINTMENT?

We are happy to return for additional help. Simply schedule another job by giving us a call or booking through our website. Standard rates will apply.

HOW AM I BILLED FOR SERVICES?

Once your appointment is complete, your Techmate will ask you to sign off on the job completed. If you are happy, we’re happy! If you are not satisfied, a manager will attempt to reach you to talk about the appointment.

WHAT IF I’M UNHAPPY WITH THE SERVICE?

We promise to get the job done right on the first visit, while providing a convenient and comfortable experience. If you are unhappy for any reason, let us know and we’ll make it right. We have a customer support team of Concierges available by phone, chat or email so you can get in touch if you ever have questions or concerns.

WHAT IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG?

Please reach out to Techmate directly via website chat, emailing us at support@Techmate.com or calling us directly at (844) 266-8694. We will do our best to resolve any issues during or shortly after the appointment.

HOW DO I MAKE CHANGES TO MY APPOINTMENT?

You can email us at support@Techmate.com or just give us a call at (844) 266-8694 with any changes to your account or job information.

WHAT IF I NEED TO CANCEL MY APPOINTMENT?

You may cancel directly from your dashboard or by calling us at (844) 266-8694 with as much notice as possible. Cancellations with less than 2 hours notice (by 7pm the previous day for jobs starting before 11am) will incur a $50 fee. This is because it is unlikely that your tech will be able to get another job in your time slot with such short notice.

WHAT IF I NEED TO RESCHEDULE MY APPOINTMENT?

If you reschedule your job with more than 2 hours notice (by 7pm the previous day for jobs starting before 11am), the cancellation fee will be suspended. If the rescheduled appointment is canceled again, a $50 fee will apply.

WHAT IF MY TECH CAN'T FIND MY HOME?

We will attempt to contact you at the phone number you've provided, and your tech will stay in your area for up to half an hour past the start time. If we've been given an incorrect address and are unable to reach you, there will be a $50 fee to your account.

CAN I REQUEST THE SAME TECHMATE FOR FUTURE APPOINTMENTS?

Absolutely! Just give us a call directly at (844) 266-8694 so that we can be sure the right tech is assigned to you.

HOW LONG IS MY SERVICE GUARANTEED FOR?

If you run into any issues with the work performed, please reach out to us. We provide a 100% guarantee on our work for 14 days. If you aren’t happy, we’ll make it right. You can reach us at support@jointechmate.com or calling us directly at (844) 266-8694.

DO YOU PROVIDE A WARRANTY FOR PARTS PURCHASED THROUGH TECHMATE?

All products are covered by the original manufacturer’s warranty and they should be contacted directly with any questions or issues.
