About Us

Our Story

In the fall of 2014, Colin received a phone call from his dad, who again had problems with his desktop computer. As a medical toxicologist, a working computer is vital to his business; however, no solution was found after working with online support for over two hours.  Dismayed by the quality of the online support, Colin looked for an on-site technical professional.  Geek Squad had terrible customer reviews, and Colin felt uncomfortable sending a stranger from Craigslist to his parent’s home. With no other options, he flew to Los Angeles the next day to fix the problem in person. After this experience, Colin realized there must be millions of other young adults with similar experiences.  Parents often rely on family members for technical help with their computers and other devices when unable to find a skilled, trustworthy professional. With technology involved in so many aspects of our lives, the availability of technical support had to change. Both being seasoned startup founders, Colin and his business partner Scott recognized the need for a dedicated and trusted technology service to connect people with trained and vetted professionals.  They created Techmate to help people set up their devices, solve computer problems, and learn how to use technology in their own home or office.

Our Mission

We passionately believe technology should be accessible to every single person, regardless of knowledge or skills. We promise to get the job done right on the first visit, while providing a convenient and comfortable experience. If you are unhappy for any reason, let us know and we’ll make it right. We have a support team of Techmates available by phone, chat or email so you can get in touch if you ever have questions or concerns.
